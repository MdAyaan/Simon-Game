INSTRUCTIONS


  1. Press any key to START. Simon will give only the first signal.
  2. Repeat Simon's signal. In this game, don't wait for Simon to repeat signals!
  3. Continue repeating the signals of the previous sequence and increasing that sequence by one. Try to reach the longest sequence.
  4. If you make an error or take more than the required time as per the difficulty to play any signal, you'll hear the "RAZZ" sound. This means the GAME is OVER.
  5. To PLAY AGAIN follow above steps.
